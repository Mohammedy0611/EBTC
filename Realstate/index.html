OUR SERVICES

Project Managment and Engineering Consultancy

We offer comprehensive engineering consultancy services in civil, mechanical, electrical, firefighting, and fire detection systems. Our certified design engineers utilize advanced software, including ETABS and STAAD Pro, to produce detailed drawings and 3D designs. Additionally, our project management consultancy covers planning, cost control, scheduling, delay analysis, budgeting, estimation, and tendering. These services are delivered by skilled engineers proficient in industry-leading tools such as SAP, Primavera (P6), and MS Project, ensuring precision and efficiency throughout your project's lifecycle.

General Contracting (Residential, Commercial, Industrial Construction)

We specialize in comprehensive general contracting services for residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects. Our experienced team manages every aspect of the construction process, from initial planning and permitting to final inspections and project completion. We collaborate closely with clients to ensure their vision is realized with precision, adhering to the highest standards of quality and safety. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail make us a trusted partner for all your construction needs.

Infrastructure Services

We specialize in the development and construction of critical infrastructure, focusing on roadworks (including asphalt, sand track, and concrete surfaces) and comprehensive pipeline systems (covering water supply, drainage, and sewer lines). Our expertise extends across a wide range of high-performance materials, such as HDPE, GRP, GRE, uPVC, CPVC, and RTRP. With a commitment to quality and durability, we ensure that all projects meet the highest industry standards, optimizing both function and longevity. Our team is dedicated to delivering efficient and sustainable solutions tailored to the specific needs of each project.

Manpower, Equipment, and Construction Material Supply

We provide skilled and unskilled manpower, construction equipment on rent, and a wide range of construction materials. Our workforce includes engineers, safety professionals, technicians, and project managers, ensuring successful project execution. We offer flexible rental services for heavy machinery and specialized tools, and supply materials like cement, steel, aggregates, concrete, and wood, sourced from trusted suppliers. Our focus on quality, reliability, and timely delivery ensures efficient project completion.

MEP Works (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing)

We provide comprehensive MEP services, including HVAC design and installation, energy-efficient electrical systems, and sustainable plumbing solutions. Our expertise covers electrical panels, cabling, transformers, renewable energy, and smart building technologies. We ensure safe and compliant water supply, drainage, and sewage systems, with greywater recycling. Fire safety services include sprinklers, alarms, and suppression systems. Additional offerings include energy audits, maintenance, retrofitting, and value engineering. Focused on quality, safety, and innovation, we deliver reliable, sustainable MEP solutions.
